Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-03

Overview

This application ("the App", "SNS_auto") is a personal automation tool operated by an individual creator to publish their own original short-form video content to their own social media accounts, including TikTok. The App is not a multi-tenant service and does not onboard third-party users.

Information We Collect

  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens issued by TikTok when the account owner authorizes the App. These are used solely to upload and publish videos to the owner's own TikTok account.
  • Basic account identifiers (e.g., open_id, username) necessary to associate uploads with the owner's account.
  • The App does not collect personal data of any other individuals, does not track end users, and does not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

How We Use Information

  • Tokens and account identifiers are used only to perform video upload and publishing actions that the account owner explicitly initiates or schedules.
  • AI-generated video content is disclosed at publish time using TikTok's content disclosure setting (is_aigc=true).

Data Storage and Security

  • All credentials are stored encrypted at rest (age encryption) on a private, owner-controlled machine.
  • Credentials are never transmitted to third parties and never displayed in plaintext logs.
  • Access tokens are short-lived (24 hours) and refreshed automatically; refresh tokens are stored encrypted.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, rent, or share any collected information with third parties.
  • Data is used exclusively for the owner's own posting workflow.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Tokens are retained only as long as needed for the posting workflow and can be revoked at any time via the TikTok account settings ("Manage app permissions").
  • Upon revocation or App removal, stored credentials are deleted.

Music Usage

When posting, the owner agrees to TikTok's Music Usage Confirmation.
